Privacy Policy

MindCapsule

Information We Collect

This app handles the following information:

  • Data stored locally — Diary content and mood records (energy, focus, fatigue, mood, sleepiness, etc.) are stored only on your device. They are not sent to our servers.
  • Purchase information collected by RevenueCat — We use RevenueCat to manage subscription (Premium) purchases and restores. Purchase status and device information may be collected and processed by RevenueCat. See the RevenueCat Privacy Policy for details.
  • Data sent to Anthropic Claude API — When you use AI features, text you enter (e.g. for summaries or analysis) is sent to Anthropic's Claude API. Use of that data is subject to Anthropic's terms and privacy policy.

Data Storage

Diary and mood data are stored on your device; we do not store them. Purchase information is managed by RevenueCat. Storage of data sent to the Claude API is governed by Anthropic's terms and privacy policy.

Analytics

We do not use advertising or analytics tools.

Third-Party Sharing

Information may be shared with the following third parties:

  • RevenueCat — For purchase and restore processing and subscription state. See the RevenueCat Privacy Policy.
  • Anthropic — For Claude API–based AI features. Text you send is processed as needed for the API and handled in accordance with Anthropic's privacy policy.
